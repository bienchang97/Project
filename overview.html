Gunpla, or "gundam plasic model", is the common name for plastic model kits depecting characters or machines in the popular anime "Mobile Suit Gundam" and all future itereations of the anime today. They are manufactured mainly in Japan and china, being imported from these two countries to the rest of the world. The first gunpla kits were soold in 1980 in Japan. Today they are being sold all around the world, being popular collectable models for anime fans who enjoy "Mecha Anime" and model builders overall.

Left Photo: Master Grade Astray Blue Frame & Astray Red Frame. Right Photo: Perfect Grade: Exia: Lightning Mode.

Gundams come in all shapes in sizes. The main three sizes are:

  • High Grade "HG 1:144"
  • Master Grade "MG 1:100"
  • Perfect Grade "PG 1:60"

HG is the smallest of the three, while PG are the largets models created. There are also exclusive models, that are made of real metal or are completely transparent. Most of those you can only get from ordering from Japan.

Getting Your First Gundam

When you purchase a gunpla, they come in fantastic large boxes with beautiful artwork of the model in action or from the anime, manga, movies or video games.

What's Inside

These boxes are huge, there's a reason behind it though. When you open your box. It will most of the time contain:

  • Runners
  • Stickers
  • Decals (Water or Dry)
  • Action Base (Only some models contain this)
  • LED kit (Only Perfect Grade or exclusive kits contain these)

Use all of these materials to create a fantastic model kit to display on your shelf! Happy building!
