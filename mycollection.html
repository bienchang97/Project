I began collection Gunpla in 2014. The first kit I owned was MG Sengoku Astray. When I was younger I always used to play with Legos and Bionicles. So I always enjoyed building and customizing figures. When I first attempted to build this model kit, it took me several days because of the large amount of pieces and the massive book of building instructions. After I finished Sengoku Astray, I fell in love with this new, harder and more mature building process. So I let go of my younger building items and invested in Gundams.
